Privacy Policy for DAO Guard by soltracker.io Discord Bot

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at soltracker@protonmail.com.

At soltracker.io we consider the privacy of our users to be extremely important. This privacy policy document describes in detail the types of personal information is collected and recorded by soltracker.io and DAO Guard bot and how we use it.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites and apps, DAO Guard Discord bot and website makes use of log files. These files merely logs visitors to the site – usually a standard procedure for hosting companies and a part of hosting services's analytics. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is used to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Consent

Information we collect

  • Server ids
  • Text channels ids and role ids (only those that are used by the bot)
  • Message ids that were created by the bot
  • Server owner id

Why we need your information and how we use it

We need the information described above to provide the basic services we offer to you.

Server ids are stored as they are used to identify the server and link settings to the corresponding server. Text channel ids used by the bot are stored to filter out channels that are not used by the bot (to avoid extra server load). Role ids are stored as bot manages specified roles for users. Message ids are stored to filter the user interactions with all messages except the one created by the bot.

WE DO NOT SHARE ANY OF THE STORED INFORMATION WITH ANY THIRD PARTIES

Data removal

All data that is connected to your server is automatically removed in 2 weeks after you kick out the bot.

Request that a business that sells a consumer's personal data, not sell the consumer's personal data. If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you.
